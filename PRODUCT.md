# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Job seekers, career changers, and professionals who need a resume quickly without signing up or sending personal data to a server. They use the app in a focused session: fill in details, preview the layout, export PDF or HTML, and leave.

## Product Purpose

A private, browser-only resume editor with live A4 preview and clean exports. Success means finishing a resume in one sitting with zero account friction and full data ownership.

## Brand Personality

Calm, capable, trustworthy. The tool should feel like a well-made desk accessory: quiet, precise, out of the way until you need it.

## Anti-references

- SaaS marketing dashboards with hero metrics and gradient CTAs
- AI-slop patterns: nested cards, uppercase section kickers on every block, numbered section eyebrows, side-stripe accents
- Account gates, onboarding wizards, and dashboard sprawl for a single-document task

## Design Principles

1. **Task-first**: Every screen serves editing or exporting; decoration never competes with the form or preview.
2. **Local by default**: Autosave, privacy, and offline behavior are visible and trustworthy, not buried in settings.
3. **Earned familiarity**: Standard product patterns (toolbar, split pane, form sections) over novelty affordances.
4. **Export fidelity**: What you see in preview is what you get in PDF/HTML.
5. **Restrained accent**: Color marks primary actions and selection, not every heading.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast on all interactive text. Keyboard shortcuts for save, export, and theme toggle. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Semantic landmarks and labeled controls throughout.
